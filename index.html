ML Emotion Detection

As a personal project, I trained a AI model to detect emotions on a persons face using OpenCV to capture what is on the camera. Learned how to prevent overfitting, actually train AI, and deploy it. I acheved 61% accuracy on my test set, and then combined 5 models with varying strengths / weaknesses to create an ensemble model. This model achived a 65% accuracy on the test data.

Credit Card Optimizer

Working with my friend Eeshan Argawal, we wanted to solve the problem of people using credit cards that dont meet their spending. Thus, we developed a optimizer that calculates, given your spending, the optimal credit card. It considers the cards perks, insurance, added benefits, points / cashback, and more. The website displays the top 3 cards which save you the most money, and how that number came to be. Learned about web design, how to work efficiently as a team, and the importantance of organization.
